What We Do

An Evening of Ideas & Dialogue

BOLD Talks brings together curious minds for evenings of thoughtful, genuine exchange.

We host authors, thinkers, and guests with something worth saying — then open the room to conversation.

"The true spirit of conversation consists more in bringing out the cleverness of others than in showing a great deal of it yourself." — Jean de La Bruyère
